I. Methods

A research stack assembled for one question:
what is actually going on?

01

Forensic accounting

Beneish M, Altman Z, Sloan accruals, Piotroski F. Run programmatically against the full XBRL filing history. Bespoke screens for industry-specific manipulation patterns.

Beneish, M. D. (1999) · Altman, E. I. (1968) · Sloan, R. G. (1996)

02

Network discovery

Multi-source connection graphs across SEC EDGAR full-text, ownership filings, Wikidata, GDELT, and federal court records. Brokerage centrality and shared-financier identification.

Helfrich, I. (2026) · deepwire, ECONSCOPE

03

Governance parsing

Structured extraction from proxy filings — special-committee composition, fairness-opinion advisors, related-party transactions, and voting-result history. DEFM14A, DEF 14A, PRER14A.

SEC EDGAR · econscope/intel/proxies

04

Regulatory and legal

OpenSanctions screening against 4.3 million global entities. CourtListener federal-docket time series. Litigation clusters, regulatory-action timelines, sanctions-watchlist resolution.

OpenSanctions, Free Law Project / CourtListener

05

Macro and sectoral

Thirty integrated data sources including FRED, BLS, BEA, Eurostat, OECD, IMF, BIS, World Bank, Comtrade. Cross-source temporal joins, correlation, and structural decomposition.

FRED, BLS, BEA, OECD, IMF, BIS · 30 adapters

06

Audit-grade reporting

Every figure carries provenance. Every pull is logged in a DuckDB audit trail. Outputs are publication-quality SVG, PDF, and DOCX, with explicit retrieval statements for archival sources.

DuckDB · pandoc · xelatex

III. Access

There is no general-access tier.

Each engagement is scoped to a specific question — a takeover, a litigation, a portfolio screen, a counterparty review — and priced against the scope, the data licensing required, and the duration of analytical support after delivery.

The underlying tooling is open and reproducible. The analytical work, the curation, and the interpretation are bespoke. Inquiries that include a name, an affiliation, and a one-paragraph description of the question receive a response within two working days.
